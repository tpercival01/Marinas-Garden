Welcome to Marina's Garden!

You've stumbled upon a beautiful, fresh garden. There are no plants, no flowers, no trees - just soft, fertile soil stretching endlessly. In the distance, a smooth stone wall catches your eye. Carved into its surface is a name: Marina. Beneath it, an inscription reads:

"This is Marina's Garden, a sanctuary of creation. To the discoverer of this sacred soil, may your care and attention bring life to this space."

Though Marina, the creator of this garden, is not here, her presence lingers in every atom of soil. The garden is now yours to tend. With the sunrise shining, you feel the responsibility settle upon you.


This website is designed to help you nurture your real-life plants by tracking their care and maintenance. It's a tool to ensure your garden thrives, just as Marina intended.


Plant Your Garden: Click on a plot of soil to open a window where you can type in the name and breed of your real-life plant. You can even give it a personal name.

Track Your Plants: The garden will track your plant's needs in real time, helping you manage watering, feeding, and general maintenance.

Receive Reminder: Set up communication methods—such as email, SMS, or social media alerts—so your plants can “reach out” when they need water, feeding, or attention.
